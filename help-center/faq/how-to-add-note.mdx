## Adding Notes

To add a new note:

1. Click the "+" button on the main page.
2. Type your text in the note box.
3. Optionally, add tags with `#tagname`.
4. Press **Save** or use **Ctrl+Enter**.

Your note appears instantly in your timeline. Use the menu to edit or delete anytime.

<Callout kind="info">
Notes are auto-synced with your flomo mind map.
</Callout>
