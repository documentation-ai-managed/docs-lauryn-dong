## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Dashboard">
    Go to <b>https://v.flomoapp.com/mine</b> and log in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the + Button">
    Tap the "+" at the bottom right to start a new note.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Note">
    Type your message, add tags if needed (e.g., <code>#idea</code>), and format as you like.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and View">
    Click <b>Save</b> to finish. The note appears in your feed instantly.
  </Step>
</Steps>
