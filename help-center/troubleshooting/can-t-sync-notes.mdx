## Problem

- Notes added aren't showing up on other devices.

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Internet connection">
    Ensure your device is online.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh the app">
    Click the refresh icon or reload the browser tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign out and back in">
    Log out of your account, then sign in again.
  </Step>
</Steps>
