## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info">
Before starting, ensure you have:
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari) or the LAURYN DONG mobile app (iOS/Android).
- An email address for account verification.
- Optional: Existing notes in Markdown, CSV, or Evernote format for import.
</Callout>

## Sign Up for an Account

Follow these steps to create your LAURYN DONG account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Sign-Up Page" icon="globe">
    Go to `https://app.lauryn-dong.com/signup`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="user">
    Provide your email, create a strong password, and complete the CAPTCHA.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Your Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification link from `no-reply@lauryn-dong.com` and click it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Onboarding" icon="check-circle">
    Set your display name and timezone. You're now ready to take notes!
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Log In and Add Your First Note

Choose your preferred platform to log in and create a note.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Web" icon="globe">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Log In">
        Navigate to `https://app.lauryn-dong.com/login`, enter your credentials.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Create Note">
        Click the `+ New Note` button. Type your content, add tags like `#first-note`, and save with `Ctrl+S` (`Cmd+S` on Mac).
      </Step>
      <Step title="View Dashboard">
        Your note appears in the feed. Use search for quick access.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Mobile App" icon="smartphone">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Download App">
        Install from App Store or Google Play: search "LAURYN DONG".
      </Step>
      <Step title="Log In">
        Open app, tap `Log In`, use your email/password.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add Note">
        Tap the floating `+` button, write your note, add emoji or tags, and swipe to save.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Import Existing Notes

<Expandable title="How to Import Notes from Other Apps" default-open="false">

LAURYN DONG supports importing from popular tools.

1. Go to Settings > Import.
2. Choose format: Markdown files, CSV, or Evernote export.
3. Upload your file (max `10MB`).
4. Map fields like title and tags if prompted.
5. Preview and confirm import.

<Callout kind="tip">
Back up your data first. Imports are one-way and preserve timestamps.
</Callout>

</Expandable>
